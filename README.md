I am an early-career researcher in cognitive neuroscience, and I am interested in how the brain learns and changes with experience, particularly during early development. My work so far has been in neuroplasticity in the human visual cortex, studying phases of heightened plasticity called sensitive periods. We do this by conducting EEG, MRI and behavioral studies with blind individuals and people who recover vision after being born with dense congenital cataracts.
